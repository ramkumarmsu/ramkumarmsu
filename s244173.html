Cryptography provides a valuable tools that can be leveraged to secure different types of communication and computing systems. This course introduces students to the fundamental underpinnings of cryptography, and several useful tools, in the form of cryptographic primitives. This course will enable students to understand I) theoretical foundations for Cryptography, ii) a brief history of cryptography, iii) modern cryptographic tools in the form of cryptographic primitives; and iv) strategies to build more complex tools by composing cryptographic protocols using basic cryptographic primitives.
